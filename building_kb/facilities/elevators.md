# Elevators

## East Elevator Bank
- Location: East side of building, all floors
- Number of elevators: 4
- Serves: All floors (1-5)
- Capacity: 12 people per elevator
- Accessible: Yes, all elevators are wheelchair accessible

## West Elevator Bank
- Location: West side of building, near cafeteria
- Number of elevators: 2
- Serves: Floors 1-3 only
- Capacity: 8 people per elevator
- Accessible: Yes

## Usage Tips
- Use east elevators for floors 4-5
- Use west elevators for quick access to floors 2-3
- During peak hours (8-9 AM, 5-6 PM), expect wait times of 2-3 minutes
- In case of fire alarm, do not use elevators - use stairs

## Accessibility
- All elevators have braille buttons
- Audio announcements for floor numbers
- Emergency call buttons connect to security
