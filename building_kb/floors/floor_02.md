# Floor 2 - Office Floor

## Overview
Floor 2 contains office spaces, meeting rooms, and collaboration areas.

## Locations

### Office Areas
- North wing: Marketing and Sales departments
- South wing: Engineering and Product teams
- Open office layout with hot desks

### Meeting Rooms
- Conference Room A: Capacity 20, near north elevators
- Conference Room B: Capacity 12, near south elevators
- Small meeting rooms (201-206): Capacity 4-6 each
- All rooms have video conferencing equipment

### Collaboration Spaces
- Open collaboration area: Center of floor
- Phone booths: Scattered throughout floor
- Quiet work zones: Near windows

### Amenities
- Coffee station: Near elevators
- Printer/copy room: Near conference rooms
- Restrooms: Near elevator banks

### Emergency Exits
- North stairwell
- South stairwell
- Emergency assembly point: Ground floor parking lot
