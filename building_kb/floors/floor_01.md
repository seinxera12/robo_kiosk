# Floor 1 - Ground Floor

## Overview
The ground floor is the main entrance level with lobby, reception, and public facilities.

## Locations

### Main Lobby
- Located at the main entrance
- Reception desk on the right side
- Information kiosk in the center
- Seating area near the windows

### Reception
- Open Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
- Visitor registration and badge pickup
- Package delivery and pickup

### Cafeteria
- Located on the west side of the building
- Open 7:00 AM - 7:00 PM
- Seating capacity: 150 people
- Near the main elevators

### Restrooms
- Men's restroom: Near the east elevator bank
- Women's restroom: Near the east elevator bank
- Accessible restroom: Near reception

### Emergency Exits
- Main entrance (north side)
- East emergency exit (near restrooms)
- West emergency exit (near cafeteria)

## Elevators
- East bank: 4 elevators serving all floors
- West bank: 2 elevators serving floors 1-3
